Welcome to (MIT)

Moya Institute of Technology, with its headquarters
located on First Avenue, Eastleigh, Nairobi, Kenya, is
a registered institution under the Technical and Vocational
Education and Training (TVET) Authority. It is committed to
providing youth with essential skills for everyday life. The
institute offers programs at Artisan, Certificate, Diploma,
and Advanced Diploma levels in Business, ICT, and Accounting
